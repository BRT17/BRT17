Ćevapi (Grilled Minced Meat)

Description: A popular Balkan street food made from a mix of ground meats, typically beef and lamb, served with flatbread and onions.

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 250g ground lamb
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Mix all ingredients thoroughly in a bowl.
  2. Shape the mixture into small, sausage-like pieces.
  3. Grill over medium heat until cooked through, about 5-7 minutes per side.
  4. Serve with flatbread and chopped onions.
Sarma (Stuffed Cabbage Rolls)

Description: A hearty dish of cabbage leaves stuffed with a mixture of meat and rice, simmered in a tangy tomato sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large cabbage
  • 500g ground pork
  • 200g rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 400ml tomato sauce
  • Salt, pepper, and paprika to taste

Directions:

  1. Boil cabbage leaves until soft.
  2. Mix ground pork, rice, onion, and spices in a bowl.
  3. Wrap the mixture in cabbage leaves to form rolls.
  4. Place rolls in a pot, cover with tomato sauce, and simmer for 1.5-2 hours.
  5. Serve warm.
Burek (Savory Pastry)

Description: A flaky pastry filled with minced meat, cheese, or spinach, perfect for breakfast or as a snack.

Ingredients:

  • 500g phyllo dough
  • 300g ground beef
  • 200g feta cheese
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 100ml yogurt
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Sauté ground beef and onion until browned, then season.
  2. Layer phyllo dough, adding the meat mixture or cheese between layers.
  3. Roll the dough tightly or arrange it in a spiral shape in a baking pan.
  4. Bake at 200°C (400°F) until golden brown, about 30 minutes.
Baklava (Sweet Pastry)

Description: A rich, sweet dessert made of layers of phyllo dough, nuts, and honey syrup.

Ingredients:

  • 500g phyllo dough
  • 300g walnuts, finely chopped
  • 200g butter, melted
  • 200g sugar
  • 250ml honey
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:

  1. Layer phyllo dough in a baking pan, brushing each layer with butter and adding nuts between layers.
  2. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 40-50 minutes until golden.
  3. Heat honey and sugar to make syrup, then pour over baked baklava.
  4. Cool completely before serving.
Moussaka

Description: A layered dish of eggplant, potatoes, and ground meat, baked with a creamy béchamel sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggplants
  • 3 potatoes
  • 500g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 300ml béchamel sauce
  • Salt, pepper, and paprika to taste

Directions:

  1. Slice eggplants and potatoes, then fry or roast until soft.
  2. Sauté ground meat with onion and spices.
  3. Layer eggplants, potatoes, and meat in a baking dish, topping with béchamel sauce.
  4. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30-40 minutes until golden.
