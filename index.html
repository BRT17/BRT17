Ćevapi (Grilled Minced Meat)

Description: A popular Balkan street food made from a mix of ground meats, typically beef and lamb, served with flatbread and onions.

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 250g ground lamb
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Mix all ingredients thoroughly in a bowl.
  2. Shape the mixture into small, sausage-like pieces.
  3. Grill over medium heat until cooked through, about 5-7 minutes per side.
  4. Serve with flatbread and chopped onions.
Sarma (Stuffed Cabbage Rolls)

Description: A hearty dish of cabbage leaves stuffed with a mixture of meat and rice, simmered in a tangy tomato sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large cabbage
  • 500g ground pork
  • 200g rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 400ml tomato sauce
  • Salt, pepper, and paprika to taste

Directions:

  1. Boil cabbage leaves until soft.
  2. Mix ground pork, rice, onion, and spices in a bowl.
  3. Wrap the mixture in cabbage leaves to form rolls.
  4. Place rolls in a pot, cover with tomato sauce, and simmer for 1.5-2 hours.
  5. Serve warm.
